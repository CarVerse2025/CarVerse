Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

We only collect the car photo(s) uploaded by the user. No name, email, or personal identifiers are required.

Uploaded images are used temporarily to generate AI-based visuals (photos or videos). We do not store this content permanently.

3. Account & Login

CarVerse does not require any login or account creation. The app can be used anonymously.

4. Subscriptions & Purchases

CarVerse offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Weekly & Monthly) and consumable credit packages. All purchases are handled securely through Apple’s in-app purchase system.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data or generated content with any third parties. All processing is handled internally.

6. User Control

Since there is no login or account, there is no personal data to delete. Any generated content is stored only on your device.
